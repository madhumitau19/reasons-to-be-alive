My reason
to be alive
is that
I'm curious
about
how
things will
change.
I wanna see my friends’ new haircuts and what kind of jobs they’ll be doing and what new things they’ll be into and who I’ll still be spending time with and who I won’t.
I wanna see if my sister will find a partner and if they'll be completely different from who she thinks she’ll be with. Everything is so temporary, but that’s pretty cool. I love surprises and I guess life is cool because it surprises you, and people are cool because they surprise you too. I’m just curious about how we’ll all turn out and that’s a good enough reason for me I think.
